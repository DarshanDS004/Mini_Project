2. Eligibility

To use MindCare India, you must:

  • Be at least 18 years of age, OR
  • Have parental/guardian consent if under 18 years
  • Be a resident of India or Indian citizen abroad
  • Have the legal capacity to enter into binding agreements
  • Provide accurate and complete information during registration

3. Description of Services

MindCare India provides:

  • Mental Health Assessment: AI-powered 27-question evaluation using machine learning (90.40% accuracy)
  • Risk Analysis: Identification of mental health risk factors
  • Personalized Recommendations: Tailored mental wellness suggestions
  • Assessment History: Tracking and monitoring mental health trends
  • Crisis Resources: Access to emergency helplines and support
  • Educational Content: Mental health awareness and resources
Free Service: All MindCare India services are provided free of charge as a Government of India initiative.

6. Medical Disclaimer

IMPORTANT: This Platform Does NOT Provide Medical Advice

MindCare India is a mental health assessment and support tool. It is NOT a substitute for professional medical diagnosis, treatment, or advice. Our AI-powered assessment is designed to provide insights and recommendations but should not replace consultation with qualified healthcare professionals.

6.1 Understanding Our Service

  • Educational Purpose: Our assessments are for informational and educational purposes only
  • Not a Diagnosis: Results do not constitute a medical or psychological diagnosis
  • Seek Professional Help: Always consult licensed mental health professionals for diagnosis and treatment
  • Emergency Situations: In crisis, contact emergency services or crisis helplines immediately

6.2 Accuracy Limitations

While our ML model achieves 90.40% accuracy, it is not 100% accurate and may produce incorrect results. Factors affecting accuracy include:

  • Honesty and accuracy of user responses
  • Individual variations in mental health conditions
  • Cultural and contextual factors
  • Model training data limitations
