Frequently Asked Questions

How accurate is the AI assessment?

Our machine learning model achieves 90.40% accuracy, trained on 5,000+ diverse mental health cases. However, it's meant to complement, not replace, professional diagnosis.

Is my data secure and private?

Yes! All data is encrypted using industry-standard protocols. We never share your personal information without explicit consent and comply with Indian data protection laws.

How often should I take the assessment?

We recommend taking the assessment monthly to track your mental health trends. However, you can take it as often as needed, especially if you notice changes in your well-being.

Is the platform accessible for persons with disabilities?

Absolutely! MindCare India is built with accessibility as a core feature. We support screen readers, keyboard navigation, and provide disability-specific assessment options.

Do I need to pay for the assessment?

No! MindCare India is completely free as part of the Government of India's initiative to make mental health support accessible to all citizens.

What if I'm in a mental health crisis?

If you're experiencing a mental health emergency, please call one of the 24/7 crisis helplines listed above immediately. Our platform also provides emergency resources in assessment results.
