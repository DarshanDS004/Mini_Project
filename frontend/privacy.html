1. Introduction

Welcome to MindCare India, a Government of India initiative under the Ministry of Health & Family Welfare. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your personal and health information.

This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you use our mental health assessment platform. Please read this policy carefully.

2. Information We Collect

2.1 Personal Information

When you register for an account, we collect:

  • Full name
  • Email address
  • Phone number
  • Date of birth
  • Gender
  • Location (state and district)
  • Disability status (optional)

2.2 Health Information

During mental health assessments, we collect:

  • Sleep patterns and quality
  • Lifestyle habits (diet, exercise, screen time)
  • Mental health indicators (stress, anxiety, depression levels)
  • Social support information
  • Work-life balance data
  • Substance use information

2.3 Technical Information

We automatically collect:

  • IP address
  • Browser type and version
  • Device information
  • Usage data and analytics

3. How We Use Your Information

We use your information to:

  • Provide Services: Deliver mental health assessments and personalized recommendations
  • AI Analysis: Train and improve our machine learning models (anonymized data only)
  • Communication: Send assessment results, alerts, and support resources
  • Crisis Response: Identify users requiring immediate mental health support
  • Research: Conduct public health research (anonymized, aggregated data)
  • Platform Improvement: Enhance user experience and accessibility features

4. Information Sharing

Important: We never sell your personal or health information to third parties.

We may share your information only in these limited circumstances:

4.1 With Your Consent

We share information when you explicitly authorize us to do so (e.g., connecting you with a mental health professional).

4.2 Legal Requirements

We may disclose information if required by law or to:

  • Comply with legal obligations
  • Protect rights and safety of users
  • Prevent fraud or security issues
  • Respond to government requests

4.3 Emergency Situations

If we identify imminent risk of self-harm or harm to others, we may contact emergency services or designated contacts.

4.4 Anonymized Data

We may share aggregated, anonymized data for research and public health purposes.

5. Data Security

We implement robust security measures to protect your information:

  • Encryption: All data transmitted between your device and our servers is encrypted using TLS/SSL
  • Secure Storage: Database encryption at rest
  • Access Control: Strict authentication and authorization protocols
  • Regular Audits: Periodic security assessments and vulnerability testing
  • Data Backup: Regular encrypted backups with disaster recovery
  • Employee Training: All staff undergo privacy and security training

6. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request copies of your personal data
  • Correction: Update or correct inaccurate information
  • Deletion: Request deletion of your account and data
  • Export: Download your assessment history and data
  • Opt-out: Unsubscribe from non-essential communications
  • Restrict Processing: Limit how we use your data

To exercise these rights, contact us at privacy@mindcareindia.gov.in

7. Data Retention

We retain your information for as long as necessary to:

  • Provide our services and support your mental health journey
  • Comply with legal obligations (minimum 5 years as per Indian healthcare regulations)
  • Resolve disputes and enforce agreements

After account deletion, we retain anonymized data for research purposes only.

8. Cookies and Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies to:

  • Maintain user sessions and authentication
  • Remember user preferences
  • Analyze platform usage and performance
  • Improve user experience

You can control cookies through your browser settings. Note that disabling cookies may affect platform functionality.

9. Contact Us

For privacy-related questions or concerns:

  • Email: privacy@mindcareindia.gov.in
  • Phone: 1800-123-456 (Toll Free)
  • Mail: Data Protection Officer, MindCare India, Ministry of Health & Family Welfare, Nirman Bhavan, New Delhi - 110011

Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of significant changes via email or platform notification. Continued use of our services after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.
