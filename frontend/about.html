About MindCare India

Empowering Mental Health Through AI-Powered Assessment and Support

Our Mission

MindCare India is a Government of India initiative dedicated to making mental health assessment and support accessible to all citizens, with a special focus on persons with disabilities. We believe that mental health is a fundamental right, and everyone deserves access to quality mental health services regardless of their physical abilities or socioeconomic status.

Our AI-powered platform leverages cutting-edge machine learning technology with 90.40% accuracy to provide personalized mental health assessments and recommendations. We combine technology with compassion to create a supportive environment for mental wellness.

Our Journey

September 2025 - Project Inception

MindCare India project launched as part of Government of India's Digital India initiative to improve mental health accessibility.

October 2025 - ML Model Development

Trained Decision Tree model on 5,000 diverse mental health cases achieving 90.40% accuracy. Focused on inclusive data representing persons with disabilities.

October 2025 - Platform Launch

Launched full-stack web platform with 27-question assessment, AI predictions, and personalized recommendations. Built with Node.js, MySQL, and Python ML integration.

Future - Expansion Plans

Planning to add video consultations, multilingual voice support, community forums, and integration with government healthcare systems.
